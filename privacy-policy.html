Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 23, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Our church youth registration application collects the following information:

  • Parent/Guardian Information: Name, phone numbers, email address, and physical address
  • Child Information: First name, last name, date of birth, gender, and any special notes
  • Attendance Records: Sign-in and sign-out times for church activities
  • Media Consent: Permission for photos/videos during church events

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Managing church youth programs and activities
  • Emergency contact purposes
  • Attendance tracking for safety and program planning
  • Communication about church events and activities

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage: All data is stored in private Google Sheets accessible only to authorized church staff
  • Access: Only designated church volunteers and staff have access to this information
  • Security: We use Google OAuth authentication to ensure only authorized users can access the data
  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • In emergency situations to ensure child safety
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as:

  • Your child participates in our programs
  • Required by law or church policy
  • Until you request deletion of your data

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you and your child
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting children's privacy. We only collect information necessary for church programs and safety purposes. Parents/guardians have full control over their child's information and can request access, correction, or deletion at any time.

8. Google Services

This application uses Google Sheets for data storage and Google OAuth for authentication. By using this application, you also agree to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
